Data Scientist

Location: Redmond, WA

Skills: Python, Machine Learning, Data Analysis

Experience: 5+ years

Salary: $130,000 - $150,000

We are seeking an experienced Data Scientist to join our team in Redmond, WA. The ideal candidate will have over 5 years of experience and be highly skilled in Python, Machine Learning, and Data Analysis. In this role, you will be responsible for analyzing complex data sets to identify trends and patterns, developing and implementing machine learning models, and providing actionable insights to drive business decisions. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand their data needs and deliver high-quality, scalable data solutions. The successful candidate will have strong problem-solving skills, excellent communication abilities, and a deep understanding of statistical analysis and data visualization techniques. The position offers a competitive salary ranging from $130,000 to $150,000, depending on experience and qualifications.
