Job Description

QA Tester

Location: Mountain View, CA

Skills: Manual Testing, Automation

Experience: 2+ years

Salary: $90,000 - $110,000

We are looking for a meticulous and detail-oriented QA Tester to join our team in Mountain View, CA. The ideal candidate will have over 2 years of experience in manual testing and automation. As a QA Tester, you will be responsible for ensuring the quality and reliability of our software products through rigorous testing and analysis. You will work closely with our development team to identify, document, and resolve issues. Your role will include creating and executing test plans, developing automated test scripts, and performing both manual and automated testing to identify defects. The successful candidate will possess strong analytical skills, excellent attention to detail, and a thorough understanding of software testing methodologies. This position offers a competitive salary range of $90,000 to $110,000, commensurate with experience and skills. Join us to help deliver high-quality software solutions and contribute to our mission of excellence in technology.
